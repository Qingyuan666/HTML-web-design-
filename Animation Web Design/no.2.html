AD 755
In the mid-term Tang Dynasty
A warlords, An Lushan 安禄山，conducted insurgence against empire Tang.
China came to a state of chaos.
War broke out everywhere, plunging the people into misery and suffering.

AD 759
The two opposing armies were facing
each other at a central city 邺城.
And me, was a girl living there while undergoing this war.

So smart me, stole the boat from the
bridge connecting death and life
and flew to the unknown opposite of this sea of time.
